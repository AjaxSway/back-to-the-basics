## The Sunday Foundation Letter (Weekly — 3-2-1 Format)
Send every Sunday 7-9 AM

**Structure:**
- 3 Principles (short thoughts)
- 2 Quotes (philosophers, thinkers, scripture)
- 1 Reflection Question

**Example:**
Subject: Three reminders for a stronger life

**3 Principles**
1. Discipline is not about intensity. It is about consistency.
2. Many problems disappear when a person returns to simple daily habits.
3. The strongest foundations are built quietly, one small decision at a time.

**2 Quotes**
"Success is nothing more than a few simple disciplines, practiced every day." — Jim Rohn
"The chains of habit are too light to be felt until they are too heavy to be broken." — Samuel Johnson

**1 Question**
Which habit in your life deserves more consistency right now?

Guarded · Grounded · Grateful
Back to the Basics Movement
